ABOUT.

Our Story

Raaj Priya Steels - the trusted name in steel and cement products! Established in 2004 with one brand in Namakkal, we have come a long way in providing top-quality steel and cement products to our customers. Today, we have over 10 brands spread across Namakkal, Thiruchengode, and Kandampalayam, making us one of the leading dealers in the district. As an authorized seller of steel and cement products, we take pride in providing our customers with only the best products that meet their requirements and exceed their expectations. Whether you're a builder, contractor, or individual homeowner, we have the products you need to get your project done right. Trust Raaj Priya Steels for all your steel and cement needs - we're here to help you build a better future!

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the trusted provider of high-quality steel and cement products, delivering exceptional value to our customers. We strive to exceed expectations by offering a wide range of reliable and sustainable materials, backed by outstanding customer service. With a focus on innovation, integrity, and continuous improvement, we aim to contribute to the success of our clients' projects and the overall growth of the construction industry. Our commitment is to build long-lasting partnerships and foster a reputation built on excellence, reliability, and integrity in everything we do.
